⚡ You Don’t Need to Hire a VP of Distribution.

Most growing CPG brands reach a point where they need serious warehousing and 3PL expertise — but not a full-time executive at $250K+ per year. Hiring a VP of Distribution or SVP of Supply Chain is a massive commitment: salary, equity, benefits, severance exposure, and the risk that it takes 6 months to figure out it’s the wrong hire.

I work on a fractional basis. I come in, assess your situation, and deliver the expertise of a seasoned senior executive without the overhead. I can set up systems and processes, negotiate your contracts, evaluate and manage your 3PL relationships, and build the operational infrastructure your company needs — then hand it off cleanly when the work is done.

No long-term employment contract. No equity ask. No disruption to your existing team. Just senior-level expertise applied exactly where you need it.
