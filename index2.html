What Goes Wrong

Most 3PL disasters aren’t dramatic. They’re slow bleeds — thousands of small failures that drain margin, frustrate customers, and show up as chargebacks, stockouts, and angry emails from your biggest accounts.

  • › Inventory accuracy drops below 95% — retail chargebacks pile up
  • › “All-inclusive” pricing hides 20+ accessorial fees
  • › Sales team overpromised; ops team can’t deliver
  • › Missed ship-by dates on Amazon, Target, Whole Foods
  • › WMS integration that was supposed to take 30 days takes 9 months
  • › Contract auto-renews with a 12-month termination window
  • › Rate increases with no benchmark to push back on
You Only Find Out After It’s Too Late

The problems above don’t show up in the sales process. They show up three months in, when switching costs six figures and six months to unwind.
